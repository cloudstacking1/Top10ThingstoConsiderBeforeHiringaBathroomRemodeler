The role of a shower liner / membrane

The shower liner or shower membrane is a waterproof barrier placed beneath tile or other finishing materials. Its job is to catch any water that penetrates the surface and channel it into the drain, rather than letting moisture seep into the structure (walls and subfloor).

  • Shower Membrane:

    • A shower membrane is a waterproof barrier installed beneath tile or other finishes to protect walls and floors from moisture. It is essential for preventing leaks, mold growth, and long-term structural damage.

  • Fiberglass Shower:

    • A fiberglass shower is a lightweight, prefabricated unit known for its affordability, easy installation, and low maintenance. It provides a sleek, smooth surface that resists stains and makes cleaning simple.
